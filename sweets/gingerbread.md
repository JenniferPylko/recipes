# Gingerbread

This fluffy gingerbread recipe takes inspiration from devil's food cake.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 4 cups flour
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup melted butter
- 3 tbsp ground ginger
- 1 tbsp allspice
- 1 tbsp cloves
- 1 tbsp cinnamon
- 1 tbsp vanilla extract
- 1 tbsp orange blossom water
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 0.25 tsp salt
- 0.5 cup buttermilk
- 1 cup molasses

## Procedure

- Preheat oven to 350F.
- Separate the eggs and beat whites to firm peaks.
- Stream 1 cup of sugar into the egg whites and beat for an additional minute.
- Combine yolks, 1 cup sugar, molasses, butter, and spices.
- Combine remaining ingredients with the yolk mixture.
- Combine the yolk and white mixtures.
- Bake for 60 minutes. Makes 2 full-size loaves.
